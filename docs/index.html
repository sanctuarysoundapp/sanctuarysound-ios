SanctuarySound

A virtual audio director that gives church production & worship teams precise mixer settings for every Sunday service.

Free & Open Source iOS 17+ Closed Beta

What It Does

🎛

Smart Gain Staging

Calculates optimal preamp gain for every channel based on source type, mic model, and your mixer's range. No more guessing.

🎼

Key-Aware EQ

Adjusts EQ recommendations per song key. Carves space between kick and bass, tames mud zones, and boosts presence where it matters.

📊

SPL Monitoring

Real-time sound level measurement with threshold alerts, haptic feedback, and session reports. Keep your mix at a healthy volume.

🔌

Live Mixer Connection

Connect to Allen & Heath Avantis and SQ consoles over TCP/MIDI. Read channel parameters in real time for delta analysis.

📅

Planning Center Import

Import setlists and team rosters directly from Planning Center Online. Songs, keys, BPM, and team positions auto-populate your service setup.

📈

Delta Analysis

Compare your actual mixer settings against computed ideals. See exactly where your mix diverges and what to adjust, channel by channel.

Built For The Church, By The Church, Free Forever.

SanctuarySound is an open-source ministry tool built to help church production & worship teams deliver great sound. No subscriptions. No paywalls. No feature gating. Every feature is free, forever.

Built by worship tech teams who understand the Sunday morning reality: rotating teams, changing bands, and the pressure of getting the mix right on the first try.
