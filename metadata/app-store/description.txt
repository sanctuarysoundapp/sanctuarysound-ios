SanctuarySound is your Virtual Audio Director for house of worship sound teams. It takes the guesswork out of live sound by calculating precise, actionable mixer settings tailored to your specific service.

WHAT IT DOES
Enter your band composition, room acoustics, vocal profiles, setlist keys, and mixer model. SanctuarySound generates gain ranges, EQ curves, compressor parameters, HPF frequencies, and fader start-points so your volunteer sound team starts every service with a solid foundation.

KEY FEATURES
- Gain staging recommendations based on your specific mixer model
- Key-aware EQ suggestions that adapt to each song's musical key
- Compressor settings with safe starting points for vocals, drums, and instruments
- SPL monitoring with real-time calibration, alerts, and session reports
- 31-band EQ analyzer (1/3-octave RTA) for room and mix analysis
- RT60 room acoustics measurement using your iPhone mic
- Planning Center Online integration for automatic setlist and team import
- Sound Engineer Q&A knowledge base with 40+ articles
- Apple Watch companion for SPL monitoring from the stage
- 5 dark themes optimized for sound booth environments

BUILT FOR VOLUNTEERS
Church sound is often run by rotating volunteers who struggle with gain staging, EQ decisions, and adapting to changing variables. SanctuarySound bridges the knowledge gap with clear, actionable recommendations.

SUPPORTED CONSOLES
